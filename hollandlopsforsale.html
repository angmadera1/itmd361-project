Holland Lop Bunnies for Sale!

Angie's Fun Farm is a small rabbitry located on the south side of Chicago, Illinois, and we currently breed Holland lops bunnies for show and pets. We strive to breed rabbits that conform to the ARBA Standard of Perfection and, most importantly, are gentle and cheerful.

Our rabbitry raises our bunnies from birth. We give them plenty of social interaction with children and other pets, as well as plenty of love and attention. We treat all of the bunnies as a part of our family, and we hope that all rabbits we raise go to a pet home that will bring them so much joy and care.
